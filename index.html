FIB Bodycam Commands

Made by Anvy

Attaching Bodycam

Attaching Bodycam (Uniform)

/me Takes out bodycam, attaches it to chest, checks its ballistic, water proof

/me makes sure it is recording and checks for the red light

/do It is recording, is ballistic and water proof

/me connects PDA to the nearest cell tower

Attaching Bodycam (Undercover)

/me takes out bodycam and attaches it to belt, hides it, checks its ballistic and water proof

/me makes sure it is recording and checks for the red light

/do It is recording, is ballistic and water proof

/me connects PDA to the nearest cell tower

Refreshing Bodycam

POV must start before refreshing bodycam, so refresh can be visible in POV.
Exception: Refresh is not required if POV starts with attaching bodycam.

/me refreshing bodycam

/do It is recording

Saving Bodycam

/me saves bodycam, uploads it to the FIB Cloud and continues recording

/do It is recording

Drone Commands

While on Duty

/me launches FIB drone

While Undercover

/me takes the FIB drone from the trunk and puts it in the backpack

/me takes the FIB drone from the backpack and launches it

/me takes the FIB drone from the ground and puts it in the backpack

Handing Footage to the Lawyer

/me saves bodycam contents onto an SD card and uploads contents to FIB servers

/me Takes SD card out of the bodycam and hands it to the lawyer

Finishing Your Shift

/me saves bodycam contents onto an SD card and uploads contents to FIB servers

/me puts the badge in the locker

Searching a Trunk

/me carefully inspects the trunk and uses a crowbar to attempt opening it

/me applies pressure with the crowbar to force the trunk open
